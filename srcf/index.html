About me

I am a PhD student at the Language Technology Laboratory at the University of Cambridge, working with Anna Korhonen. My research examines how we can build equitable language technologies through cheap, small, and specialized language models.

Previously, I was a predoctoral researcher at the Allen Institute for AI and an engineer at Explosion working on spaCy.
