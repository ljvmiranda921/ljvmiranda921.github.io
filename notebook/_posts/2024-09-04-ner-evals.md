<span class="firstcharacter">I</span>t has been a year since I released [calamanCy](https://github.com/ljvmiranda921/calamancy) and its accompanying BERT-based models.
They were pretty good at the time, even surpassing LLM performance ([Miranda, 2023](https://aclanthology.org/2023.sealp-1.2.pdf)).
However, newer versions of GPT were released, and recent LLMs even boast multilingual capabilities.
I guess it's high time to refresh my evals and post the result here.

In this blog post, I will evaluate various LLMs on the TLUnified-NER ([Miranda, 2023](https://aclanthology.org/2023.sealp-1.2.pdf)) and Universal NER ([Mayhew et al., 2024](https://aclanthology.org/2024.naacl-long.243/)) datasets.
The former focuses solely on Tagalog, my native language, while the latter consists of 13 diverse languages.
