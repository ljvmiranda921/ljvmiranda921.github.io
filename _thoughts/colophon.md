A first thought, on how this site is put together.

It's a static site built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and hosted on GitHub Pages. The type is [Nanum Myeongjo](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Nanum+Myeongjo) for body text, [Junicode 2](https://github.com/psb1558/Junicode-font) for the italic display lettering, and Berkeley Mono for code. The palette is warm paper and brick red with a teal accent — and there's a light/dark toggle hiding in the clock on the homepage.

Thoughts here are short and half-formed. For longer pieces and my newsletter, there's my [Substack](https://jenniferjiangkells.substack.com).
