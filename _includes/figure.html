{%- comment -%} Figure with a LaTeX-style caption: small-caps lead-in, then the body. Usage: {% include figure.html src="/assets/images/foo/bar.png" width="800" title="Figure 1." caption="Some *markdown* caption with [links](https://example.com)." alt="Alt text for the image" %} src and caption are required; width, title, and alt are optional. {%- endcomment -%}
